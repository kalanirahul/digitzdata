Who We Are

Actuaries, technologists, and strategists united by a purpose

DD Consulting is a UAE-headquartered advisory firm with a global footprint spanning the Middle East, Asia, Europe, and Australia. We combine deep technical expertise in actuarial science, accounting, and technology with a hands-on, partnership-driven approach. When we see a gap in the market, we build tools to fill it—like Reserium, our IBNR reserving platform born from years of practical experience.

Our Vision

Our Story

Founded in 2019, DD Consulting emerged from a simple belief: that actuarial excellence and technological innovation should go hand in hand.

What started as a boutique actuarial consultancy in the UAE has grown into a multi-disciplinary advisory firm serving clients across the Middle East, Asia, Europe, and Australia.

We think smart, not just hard. We leverage sophisticated tools and contemporary methodologies rather than conventional approaches.

Our Approach

We don't just advise—we build. When we see a gap in the market, we create tools to fill it. Our Reserium platform for IBNR reserving is a prime example: born from years of hands-on actuarial work, now serving clients globally.

We are everywhere, yet together. Our remote-first operations enable seamless client availability across different time zones.

Communication is fundamental. We prioritize accessibility and clarity as central to our value proposition.
